ABOUT PRODUCT APP

Taking the current menstrual pad support box as the starting point, this project aims to design a more systematic and UX-oriented intelligent public facility to support sustainable operation and provide women with more benefits.

Each menstruation is the traction of the moon on the tide and you.
—— Stand By Her

PRODUCT

The support box aims at breaking the menstrual stigma and providing women in need with pads. It carries poetic design semantics, trying to express support and care for female groups from the appearance design. It provides a better user experience, eliminates their concerns about hygiene and allergies issues, and solves the problem of unsustainable operations.

HOW TO USE TIDE?

1.Find the support box
(through App).

2.Hold the phone close to the “moon” (Scan the QR code or NFC).

3.Get the pad from the “tide”.

4.Pay for the pad on App later or join activities on the App to “pay” for the pads.

APP

In the platform, the functions related to the support box are mainly telling users basic information about the box and pads. It is not an application that only has these simple functions. More useful modules are added to enrich the content of the App, aiming to increase user engagement and stickiness, and make them willing to contribute to eliminating stigma and creating social impact.
